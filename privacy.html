PRIVACY
POLICY

At Arka Lens, we respect your privacy as much as we respect the art of visual storytelling. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as when you fill out our contact form or subscribe to updates. This includes:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number).
  • Project details and event dates.
  • Any other information you choose to share in your messages.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the purpose of business communication and service delivery. Specifically:

  • To respond to your inquiries and booking requests.
  • To draft contracts and invoices for services rendered.
  • To improve our website functionality through anonymous analytics.

3. Media & Portfolio Rights

As a visual arts studio, photographs and videos captured during booked sessions may be displayed in our portfolio (website and social media) to showcase our work, unless a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) or specific privacy clause has been signed prior to the shoot.

4. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies to ensure smooth navigation and performance (such as the loading animations you see). We do not use aggressive tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at Arkalens.Films.
